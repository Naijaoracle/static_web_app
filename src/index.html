Welcome to My Digital Health & AI Portfolio

Hello and welcome! I'm David Dasa, a Medical Doctor on a path to explore the intersection of healthcare and technology. Currently pursuing an MSc in Digital Health & Artificial Intelligence, I seek to leverage technology's potential to enhance healthcare solutions.

With years of clinical experience, I've navigated high-pressure medical situations, leading cardiac arrest responses and coordinating postoperative care. My journey is fueled by a vision to integrate innovative technology into healthcare practice, aiming to create impactful solutions to real-world challenges.

From conducting research on the use of blockchain technology in healthchare, to organizing medical outreach missions. I am committed to innovation and patient-centric care.

Join me in exploring the dynamic realm where medicine meets technology, as we strive to shape the future of healthcare.

Featured Project

Remote patient monitoring system

A project to build a remote patient monitoring system which uses short range bluetooth low energy medical devices, verifies proximity to a trusted device, and sends the data to medical professionals who verify the data is from a trusted device, saving it to a distributed ledger running a proof of authority consensus protocol.
