Volunteering Experience

Rural Medical/Surgical Outreach, 2016.

In 2016, while serving as the Mission Secretary of my schools chapter of the Christian Medical and Dental Association Nigeria - Students, I played a leading role in organising a medical outreach to a rural community in another state.

The participants included surgeons, nurses, community physicians, medical students, and others. About 610 patients received free medical consultations and about 50 patients received free surgeries.

Prior to this I had participated in outreaches, but this one also gave me an opportunity to take a leadership role. Ultimately it was a great opportunity to make a real difference in the lives of those who needed it but were unable to find solutions.
