Blood Pressure Prediction

Predicting blood pressure in healthcare is vital for early detection and management of cardiovascular diseases. This project utilizes advanced machine learning models to forecast blood pressure based on patient features.

In this phase, we train a random forest model, support vector machine, and gradient boosting machine to predict cardiovascular disease.

Healthcare Relevance:

Accurate blood pressure prediction plays a key role in preventing life-threatening cardiovascular conditions. Timely predictions allow for personalized treatments to mitigate risks associated with these conditions.

Benefits:

  • Early Intervention: Prediction models enable early identification of at-risk individuals.
  • Personalized Treatment: Accurate forecasts aid in tailoring treatments to individual needs.
  • Resource Optimization: Efficient predictions assist in effective resource allocation.

Conclusion

The notebook above showcases the use of three models to predict cardiovascular disease, showing closely matched performance. In the next phase, we aim to explore neural network models and assess their performance.

Further iterations and fine-tuning are underway to enhance predictive precision and generalizability.
